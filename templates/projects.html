MARC

Me and two other students made a VHDL implementation of the Core Wars 88 standard. Core Wars is basically a computer game where two programs compete and try to destroy each other. Read the wiki for more info. We designed a microprogrammed processor with a Redcode assembler and wrote some warriors for it.
